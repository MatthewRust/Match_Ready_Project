About MatchReady

MatchReady is a Django-based web application designed to centralize sports team management, communication, and scheduling for players, coaches, and fans.

Key Features:

  • User Roles: Separate access and features for Players, Coaches, and Fans.
  • Team Management: Coaches can create teams with unique IDs; Players and Fans can join teams.
  • Team Sheets: Coaches manage player availability for matches; Players and Fans can view published sheets.
  • Announcements: Coaches post important updates; Players and Fans stay informed.
  • Match Schedules: Public view of upcoming matches and team-specific schedules.

Benefits:

  • Players: Never miss a match or important announcement. Access team info easily.
  • Coaches: Save time managing schedules, availability, and communication.
  • Fans: Stay engaged with easy access to match schedules and team news.
